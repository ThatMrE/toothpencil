🗣️ Brand Voice

Toothpencil speaks like your funniest friend who also happens to care deeply about the planet. We're quirky, punny, warm, and eco-positive — never preachy, never boring.

😄 Quirky

We're a toothbrush shaped like a pencil. Our entire existence is quirky. We lean into the absurdity and make people smile before they even start brushing.

🤣 Punny

We love wordplay. Pencil puns, tooth puns, school supply humor. If there's a pun to be made, we've already made it. "Sharpen Your Smile" is just the beginning.

🌱 Eco-Positive

We don't guilt-trip. We celebrate. Every brush is a tiny party for the planet. We talk about what we're planting, not what others are destroying.

🤗 Warm

We're inviting, inclusive, and approachable. Like finding a hand-written note in your lunchbox. We make sustainability feel like a hug, not a lecture.

📢 Ad Campaign Concepts

From absurdist humor to earnest eco-messaging — campaigns that make people stop, laugh, and switch.

🦷 "Pencils Made of Teeth" — Absurdist Campaign

A fake documentary exploring the "discovery" that pencils are made of teeth. Museum exhibits. Academic experts. Dental conspiracy theories. Then the twist: "Actually, we did the opposite. We made a toothbrush out of a pencil." Cut to product shot with a wink.

Formats: TikTok series (6 episodes), YouTube pre-roll, Instagram Reels, billboard teasers ("PENCILS MADE OF TEETH?" → reveal)

📚 "School Supplies" — Back to School

Toothpencil shows up in pencil cases, desk organizers, and school supply lists. "The only school supply that fights cavities." Seasonal Q3 campaign aligned with back-to-school shopping.

Formats: Social, retail POP, Target endcap

🌍 "Outlive" — Eco Impact

Side-by-side: your plastic toothbrush in 2426 (still in a landfill) vs. your Toothpencil in 2027 (already a tree). Stark. Simple. Shareable.

Formats: Billboard, transit ads, print

🎬 "Morning Edit" — Lifestyle

Beautiful morning routines — but the toothbrush is a pencil. Coffee, stretch, brush with a pencil. "Wait, what?" Aspirational aesthetic meets double-take humor. Targets wellness/lifestyle audience.

Formats: TikTok, Pinterest, Instagram

🏭 "Made in America" — Factory Story

Behind-the-scenes at General Pencil Company. 5th generation, last pencil factory in America, now making the future of toothbrushes. Craft, heritage, and innovation in Jersey City.

Formats: Long-form video, PR, editorial

✏️ "Sharp Reviews" — UGC

Customers rate their brushing experience with letter grades. "A+ for making me feel like a kindergartener again." "B+ because my roommate keeps stealing it." Authentic, funny, shareable.

Formats: Social carousel, email, website
